Studying for an Amateur Radio licence, hopefully I'll have one by the end of February or March.
The Amateur Radio Foundation Licence allows operation of up to 25W radios on UHF, VHF, and HF bands. To get it, you need to pass a 26-question multiple-choice paper with over 70%.
If you want to get in to Ameture radio i would suggest getting an sdr or a lora mesh node.
There are 3 levels of Amateur Radio Licences:
1) Foundation
2) Intermediate
3) Full
